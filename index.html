Don't Make Me Think, Revisited: A Common Sense Approach to Web Usability Rocket surgery made easy: the do-it-yourself guide to finding and fixing usability problems.

Welcome to the summary of book.

Ch-1

Self-evident:-
=>In your website all webpage should be self-explanatory.Do not make user to think about next step.

=>When you are doing something that requires originality then go for "Tips" for the users who are unaware of your webpage topic.

Ch-2

Scanning through webpage:-
=>User spend very little time on reading whole page and just scan the webpage and reads whatever his eyes catches the most.

=> Most of the time user do not look for reasonable option, insted of he goes for the best option.

=> One of the surprising outcome of the usability test: User do not concern about the way of getting their information.As far as they are getting information they would not bother about better way to do it.

Ch-3

Appearance of Webpage:-

=>A webpage should have content in heirarchical order,related things should put in similar visual style,things should be "nested".

=> Every webpage should be conventional manner i.e take look of other websites and then create webpage.If you have better idea that user may appreciate then only go for unconventional way.

=> Divide your webpage in certain way that user can easily decide that which part is important in which can be neglected.

=>Apperance of webpage means not only good look but user must be able to identify the clickable thing on webpage.

Ch-4

Choices:-

=> Do not give user so much choices that makes him to click number of times.Make your webpage by which with minimum clicks user gets whatever he is lookig for.A the same time user do not mind to click more times if he is on the right track and getting things step by step after every click.

=>Provide choice with which in very less click user gets desired output.

h-5

Avoid use of Needless words:-

=> Do not use unwanted words in your webpage, that is just taking up space of your webpage.Less reduces the noise of webpage and makes useful content more notable.

=> Instructions is major thing that no one is going to read.Always eliminate lengthy instructions or arrange them in very short terms.

Ch-6

Navigation:-

=>Make your webpage on which user can easily navigate for required information.If user does not find what he is looking for he will leave that webpage.

=>Sections and Utilities are two main component of webpage.Sections are the links for the main sections available on webpage.Utilities are the element of webpage that are not really content but provides information about webpage.

Ch-7

Creating Homepage:-

=>The homepage of website tells user about your website,so home page needs to have content in heirarchy which may provide registration,search,navigation for webpage,etc.

=>There must be two elements on your webpage:Tagline and The welcome blurb. Both works to explain in more depth about website to user.

=> Navigation on homepage is as important as on other pages.Homepage is the page where user comes back if he do not get what he is looking for.

Ch-8

Likes and Dislikes of User:-

=>Your webpage should be appealing and connect user to your webpage.Irrelevant to the choice of web developer and designer webpage should be attractive and easy for user.Both developer and designer can work on thier terms and make this thing happen.Both are equally important for creating webpage.

Ch-9

Testing of website:-

=>You don't need someone who charges big amount fortesting of your website.You only need one person or group of people who discuss their views and ideas on webpage.You need people who has gone through so many webpages that knows what works and what doesn't for users.

=>Testing of your webpage give you clear picture of what you need to do next.Which next step you need to perform for creating webpage.

Ch-10

Courtesy on Webpage:-

=> User of your webpage should be treated with courtesy.You webpage should be filled with pool of goodwill for the user.That makes user to use continue on your website.

=>Do not try to hide information that user requires the most on your webpage.Do not ask for information that really don't required from the user.Your site should be organizes and professional or it will make user to discontinue use of your website.

=>Put that thing straight forward what user wnat to do on yoursite.Reduce the steps for user to remember everything.Provide FAQ service for the user.Provide user service friendly pages.Make it easy for user to recovr if he makes nay mistake on using your website.

Ch-11

Accessibility:-

=>There are two main concern for accessibility:More work and Compromise in design.There should be an accessibility service in your webpage to increase the number of users.

=>To increase accessibility fix the problem that confuse everyone,use CSS and other many more experiments.

Ch-12

Never say Never:-

=>Never stop working on your webpage in terms of make it more interesting.Never stop trying new thing on your website just because it is not used before or it is not appealing enough on initial stage for rest of people.
